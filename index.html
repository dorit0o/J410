What is the Yang Gang?

Andrew Yang announced his campaign for president in 2017. Within that two year span, Yang has grown a following that has some of the most interesting people. Tech guru's like Elon Musk, truckers in the midwest and even the meme lords of Reddit have all joined the "Yang Gang."

The SLO Yang Gang is small, but it draws people from many different parts of San Luis Obispo. From entrepreneurs, to former Republicans, to mothers and most prevelantly the students, all have joined the SLO Yang Gang in one way or another.
